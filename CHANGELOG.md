# Changelog

## v2.1.0 - Hub-Based UI Release
- Added official hub assignment field to every submarket.
- Applied hub-based color model:
  - Alabama Hub: orange family
  - Pensacola Hub: blue family
  - Panama City Hub: green family
  - Growth Markets: gray family
- Added Submarket Hubs panel with hub counts and click-to-zoom behavior.
- Added collapsible sidebar to increase map workspace.
- Reworked Market Summary to focus on business categories instead of GIS coordinates.
- Moved version/release details into the About section rather than the main header.
- Kept Health Score framework and expanded search architecture.
